UN sustainability goals

Goal 2: End Hunger, Achieve Food Security and Improved Nutrition and Promote Sustainable Agriculture.
Our project contributes to Goal 2 by aiming to fulfill two sub targets:

Protecting Small Farmers:

By prioritizing the support and growth of small-scale farmers we intend to enhance local food production and food security.

Preserving Genetic Diversity in Crops

We are committed to preserving and promoting genetic diversity in crops to ensure long-term agricultural sustainability.

Goal 11: Make Cities and Human Settlements Inclusive, Safe, Resilient and Sustainable:

Our application focuses on creating an inclusive platform that connects urban and rural communities while promoting sustainable food practices.
Furthermore, our application promotes safe and resilient food supply chains within cities and settlements.
The exact sub target that we intend to fulfill in this goal is:

Keep city air clean:

By avoiding transportation of produce that is also grown in our local area, e.g. the cheapest grapes in supermarkets are often imported from the south of europe,
however, local farmers can sell their locally grown grapes from home they are able to compete with the price in supermarkets.
Furthermore, it is much more beneficial for the environment if the produce is locally grown and unnecessary transportation is avoided.

Goal 12: Ensure Sustainable Consumption and Production Patterns:

We contribute to Goal 12 by encouraging users of our platform to make informed choices regarding the consumption of locally sourced and sustainable food products.
By supporting sustainable farming and hunting practices, we aim to ensure the responsible production of food.
The subtarget we intend to fulfill is the following:

Halve per capita global food waste at retail and consumer level:

By being able to sell your locally grown produce (or hunted meat) that you are not able to consume you reduce unnecessary waste.

Sell a product:

In order to sell a product all you have to do is press the 'Sell a product' button, located in the top-left corner of the navigation bar.
By pressing the button you will be redirected to a form. In the form you will first register as a seller by filling out your personal information.
Next you will add the information about the product you want to sell.
If you already are a seller ?(ta bort) you will still have to fill in the information and after that you can add an additional product to sell.
After this your registration is complete, your ad will be displayed on the website.

Browse local sellers:

The different local sellers are all located on a map on the front page. By clicking on a pin you will see additional information about that perticular seller.
A selection of ads containing information about location, product sold and price, will also be displayed in the column on the lefthand side of the page.
The searchbar above the selection of ads can be used to search for ???. Next to the search bar there is also a filter button that can be used to
display only a certain category and/or item. In order to filter on a certain item a category needs to be chosen first, e.g. to display only the ads containing the
item 'Bananas' you will first have to choose the category 'Fruits' thereafter you can choose the item 'Bananas'.
You can also choose to filter only on a category, e.g. by choosing the category 'Fruits' and then pressing the filter button.
All(?) ads in that category will be displayed in the left hand column and only their locations will be displayed on the map (?).
